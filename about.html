The Story Behind Sam

Built in Central Mass for the people who keep the lights on.

I started Response Ready AI because I was tired of time after time not getting through to make an appointment I needed for a leaky roof. I called around. I waited for a few days. I dumped out a bucket a few times.

By the time the first guy got back to me, I'd already gone with a different one - simply becasue they had actually picked up the phone.

I realized that the ones who hadn't picked up weren't ignoring me. They didn't think they were too good for a new customer. They were busy working and just weren't able to answer my call.

I built Sam to fix that.
